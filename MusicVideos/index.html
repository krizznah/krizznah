'Aubergine' by Armand Hammer

I was inspired by Kiyoshi Kurosawa's 'Cure' which led to me making this video. The concept of the video revolves around hypnosis, as can be seen in the film. It's also riddled with clues as to what had been on my mind while I was making this video. Armand HAmmer are a Hip-Hop duo who also serve as a huge inspiration to the way I think and create, and therefore, Aubergine off of their 2021 record, 'Haram' seemed like the best way to go.
